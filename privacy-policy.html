Shamatha Privacy Policy

Shamatha is designed as a quiet, contemplative meditation application. We intentionally collect as little user data as possible.

Data Collection

Shamatha does not sell personal data. Shamatha does not use invasive tracking systems.

Meditation progress, reflections, and session information are primarily stored locally on your device.

Analytics

At this stage, Shamatha does not use third-party advertising systems.

Permissions

The application may use basic device functionality required for meditation session playback and app operation.

Changes

This privacy policy may evolve as the application develops.
